What is LVH?

The term Hackathon is a combination of the words "hack" and "marathon". It refers to an event where where like-minded programmers, UX UI (User Experience Design) designers, researchers and innovators assemble. Hackathons aim to build tech solutions for social impact or educational purposes. The Limpopo Varsity Hackathon is open to students from University Of Venda, University Of Limpopo & Tshwane University Of Technology.

NO APPLICATION FEE REQUIRED.

Objectives

The objectives of the Hackathon is in response to our quest to stimulate, educate, up-skill, equip and preapare the youth of Limpopo to adopt quickly to the future of work and business and for policy makers and industry to enable a conductive environment for an inclusive economy.

Benefits


Hackathon gather talented and skilled developers and researchers who will demonstrate their talents and capabilities to future employers or clients.

Stoking creativity, accelerating focused problem solving, learning, sharing skills, branding, retaining employees, and future proofing a business.

Skill development, networking, and personal growth.

Building strategic partnership for a sustainable and inclusive digital economy.
